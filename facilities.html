Sports

Physical education lessons and sports fixtures are conducted in the large tarmac area which has 2 volleyball courts and basket ball courts and also on the sports field.

Codes of sports offered at the school include:

Football, Volleyball, Athletics, Table tennis, Beach volley ball, Scatting and Horse Riding , shuttle cock etc.,

Learning Resource Center

The focus here will be on guided learning and self learning. The LRC contains a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books, folks, fairy tales, magazines, periodicals and reference material for students and staff. It will be computerized, fully equipped with CD Rom work stations. Printers and staffed with qualified librarians to facilitate the work of students and staff.

Silence

Strict silence is to be observed in the class room, dining hall, library and office premises also while going and coming from class rooms and lining up for morning assembly.
