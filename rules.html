RULES

General Rules

All the students are expected to speak only in English in the classrooms and campus.
Pupils are responsible for the safety of their own belongings. Valuable ornaments (gold, silver etc.) Should not be worn in the school. The school will not be responsible for the loss of the same. Cleanliness in person and dress should be highly observed. Books should be kept neat and tidy.
When a pupil requires leave from the class the parent should apply to the class teacher and permission should be obtained. In the case of an emergency or sudden illness the pupil should bring the leave letter, signed by the parents, the next working day.
All the students are expected to be in school by 8.45 a.m. The school begins at 9.00 a.m. with the assembly and ends at 4.10 p.m. All the students and teachers are expected to be present for the assembly. Disciplinary action will be taken against those who come late repeatedly. If any school equipment or belongings of other pupils are destroyed or damaged by the pupil. He will have to pay for it. Parents are requested to respond to the invitation from the school for public functions.
Pupils are not allowed to enter other class rooms at any time.

Rules regulations

Parents and guardians are requested to co-operate with the school in the observance of all rules regulations laid down below and those announced, by the school authorizes as and when they are required. Parents are requested to keep in touch with the Head of the institution and teachers regarding the progress, attendance and conduct of their children.

Communication

Parents and guardians are requested to mention the standard and division of their child in their correspondence to the school. They are also requested to inform the school authorities. The inability to participate in any of such meetings should be informed early.

Meetings

Parents and guardians are requested to participate in the parent teacher meetings arranged by the school authorities. The inability to participate in any of such meetings should be informed early.

Cash and valuables

Children are advised not to bring cash and valuables to the school. If a child brings valuables to school and loses it or is robbed, the school authorities will not be held responsible.
